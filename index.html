Airplane IoT

2022

RHU university

Our networking project involves the development of full topology of a new feature for commercial airplanes, where data from airplane sensors such as wind speed, height, airplane speed, and error codes are saved in the airplane black box and then sent to a database server using a satellite connection. The database server can track the location of the airplane and, in case of any technical issues, notify the watch tower immediately with a beware message sent from the server. This feature ensures that the watch tower is aware of any issues in real-time, improving the safety and security of air travel.

Human-vs-AI snake game

2022

RHU university

In our human-vs-AI snake game, we used the best algorithm to run the snake and tried many algorithms until we got the highest score. The A* algorithm was used where the computer calculates the distance between the snake head and the fruit that it must eat, and chooses a path corresponding to two factors: distance and if the way is blocked by the human snake or a wall. Each time the snake eats a fruit, its length increases, and the game ends when the snake hits itself, the human player's snake, or a wall. With our advanced algorithm, the AI snake was able to compete with the human player and provide a challenging and exciting gaming experience.
